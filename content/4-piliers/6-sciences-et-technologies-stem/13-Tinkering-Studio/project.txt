Mission: Experiments with science, art, technology, and delightful ideas.

Objectifs: We design opportunities for people to “think with their hands” in order to construct meaning and understanding.

----

Jeunes: Entre autres

----

Description: Espace d'exparimentation du centre de sciences de SF (exploratorium).  a studio workshop for playful invention, investigation, and collaboration The Tinkering Studio is an immersive, active, creative place at the Exploratorium where museum visitors can slow down, become deeply engaged in an investigation of scientific phenomena, and make something—a piece of a collaborative chain reaction—that fully represents their ideas and aesthetic.

Moyens: Principes :  - Activity Design : Thoughtful approaches to interacting with materials, tools, and technologies. - Environmental Design : Guides for the design and use of the studio setting. - Facilitation : Principles that inform the interaction between staff and museum visitors.

Facteurs: - 'learning studio' séparé pour préparer les activités  - partenariats avec des 'tinkerers' qui viennent aider à planifier et animer les activités - théorie 'constuctivist'
