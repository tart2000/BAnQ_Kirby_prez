Title: Hitech LAB

En: 6

Typologie: STEM education lab

----

Mission: The road to a STEM career -  Where Teens and Technology Meet:  Engaging teens with digital media

----

Objectifs: Founded on innovative teaching methods which demonstrate that teens learn most effectively through hands-on projects and peer-to-peer communications, HiTech provides curriculum that is both self-paced and structured, offering an array of classes and projects for youth to select. Teaching subjects are: Genetics: Biology and Nanotechnology; Gamification; Advanced Robotics; Wearable Technologies; and Augmented Reality

----

Jeunes: oui

----

Description: Envisioned as a launching point for the STEM career pipeline, HiTechfocuses on producing the next generation of scientists, mathematicians, technologists, and engineers needed in the community and nationwide.
